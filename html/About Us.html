About Us

Kopano Manyano God the Founder Center for Homeless is a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming lives by providing shelter, food, and comprehensive support services to homeless individuals and families in Gauteng Province, South Africa. Our mission is to restore dignity, promote independence, and enhance the quality of life for those in need.

Founded with a vision of compassion and community support, we rely on the generosity of donors, volunteers, and strategic partners to sustain our operations. Our work is driven by a deep commitment to social welfare, public health, and sustainable development, ensuring that every individual has the opportunity to thrive.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide immediate relief and long-term solutions to homelessness through shelter, nutrition, healthcare, and vocational training, empowering individuals to rebuild their lives with dignity and purpose.

Vision: A community where no one is left without a home, hope, or opportunity, fostering inclusivity and self-sufficiency for all.

Our Journey

2015: Founded

Kopano Manyano was established with a vision to support the homeless in Gauteng.

2018: First Shelter Program

Launched our first shelter, providing safe haven for 50 individuals.

2022: Expanded Food Assistance

Served over 5,000 meals to those in need across the province.

2025: Ongoing Impact

Continuing to empower lives with comprehensive support services.
