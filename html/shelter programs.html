Shelter Programs

At Kopano Manyano Center, our Shelter Programs provide safe, stable housing to help individuals and families transition out of homelessness. We offer temporary shelters, transitional housing, and support to secure permanent residences, empowering residents to rebuild their lives with dignity.

Our Approach

We create a supportive environment with access to basic amenities, security, and personalized case management. Our programs include:

  • Emergency Shelters: Immediate safe housing for those in crisis, available 24/7.
  • Transitional Housing: Medium-term residences with support services to prepare for independent living.
  • Permanent Housing Support: Assistance with securing affordable housing and financial planning.
  • Case Management: Tailored plans to address individual needs, including job training and healthcare access.

Our goal is to provide not just a roof, but a pathway to stability and self-sufficiency.
