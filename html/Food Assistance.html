Food Assistance Program

Nourishing hope and health for the homeless in Gauteng with daily meals and sustainable food solutions.

Our Mission

At Kopano Manyano Center, our Food Assistance Program is dedicated to eradicating hunger among the homeless in Gauteng. We serve daily nutritious meals, provide food security, and empower individuals through education and community support.

How We Make a Difference

  • Community Kitchens: Hot, balanced meals served daily at shelters and outreach centers.
  • Food Parcels: Non-perishable packages for long-term food security.
  • Nutrition Workshops: Education on healthy eating and meal planning.
  • Food Drives: Community-led collections to sustain our pantry.
