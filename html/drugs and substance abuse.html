Path to Recovery

Kopano Manyano Center provides tailored support for individuals struggling with drugs and substance abuse, particularly among the homeless population. Our services include:

  • Rehabilitation Programs: Structured programs to support detox and recovery.
  • Counseling and Therapy: One-on-one and group sessions to address underlying issues.
  • Relapse Prevention: Education and strategies to maintain sobriety.
  • Community Reintegration: Support to rebuild lives and reconnect with society.

We are committed to helping individuals achieve lasting recovery and regain their dignity.
