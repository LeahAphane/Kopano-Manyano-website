Helping Victims Heal

At Kopano Manyano Center, we provide comprehensive support for individuals affected by crime, particularly those experiencing homelessness. Our services include:

  • Counseling Services: Professional trauma counseling to address emotional and psychological impacts.
  • Safety Planning: Personalized plans to ensure safety and prevent further victimization.
  • Legal Support Referrals: Connections to legal aid for navigating the justice system.
  • Community Support Groups: Peer-led groups to foster healing and resilience.

Our goal is to empower victims to regain control of their lives and find a path to stability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can access your counseling services?

Our counseling is available to anyone affected by crime, with priority for homeless individuals in Gauteng.

Are your services confidential?

Yes, all counseling and support services are strictly confidential to protect your privacy.
