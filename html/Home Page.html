Join Our Mission

Kopano Manyano is a non-profit organization committed to restoring the dignity and potential of homeless and vulnerable individuals in Gauteng and beyond. Our mission is to provide more than just shelter and food — we aim to empower individuals with the tools, care, and guidance necessary to transition from crisis to stability, and from dependency to self-reliance. By fostering a supportive environment and holistic development programs, we help people reclaim their lives and contribute meaningfully to society. We believe in hope, humanity, and second chances.

Our Key Services

👤

Counseling for Abuse Survivors

Compassionate support for individuals affected by abuse to help them heal and rebuild their lives.

👨🏽

Albinism Awareness

Educational programs to promote understanding, reduce stigma, and support people with albinism.

💊

HIV & AIDS Counseling

Confidential counseling and guidance for individuals affected by HIV/AIDS and their families.

💊

Drug Abuse Counseling

Professional support and recovery programs to help individuals overcome drug addiction.

👥

Community Outreach

Engaging with communities to raise awareness and provide direct assistance to those in need.
