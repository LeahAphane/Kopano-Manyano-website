Community Engagement

At Kopano Manyano Center, our Community Engagement Programs are designed to foster connection, collaboration, and empowerment through dynamic events, workshops, and outreach initiatives, building a stronger, more inclusive community in Gauteng.

Building Bridges

We are committed to uniting people in support of our mission to end homelessness. Our initiatives include:

  • Community Events: Organizing clean-up drives, charity runs, and awareness campaigns to bring residents together.
  • Workshops: Providing skill-sharing sessions on financial literacy, mental health, and personal development.
  • Outreach Programs: Collaborating with schools and local businesses to educate and inspire meaningful action.
  • Volunteer Opportunities: Engaging community members in impactful roles to support our programs.

Our vision is to create a vibrant, inclusive community where every individual plays a part in making a lasting difference.
