About Kopano Manyano

Kopano Manyano Center is a visionary nonprofit dedicated to eradicating homelessness in Gauteng through innovative programs, compassionate care, and community collaboration.

Our mission is to restore dignity, foster resilience, and build a brighter future for all with a legacy of impact.

Our Services

Shelter Solutions

  • Secure, safe housing for immediate relief and long-term stability.
  • 24/7 emergency shelters with comprehensive support.
  • Transitional housing programs to ensure sustainable independence.

Nutrition Programs

  • Daily distribution of nutritious, balanced meals.
  • Collaborative food partnerships to combat hunger.
  • Health and wellness support for individuals experiencing homelessness.
